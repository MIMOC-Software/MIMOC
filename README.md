# MIMOC

**MIMOC** — Modular IT Management Orchestrated Console — is an on-premise, zero-agent and modular IT management console for network discovery, asset inventory, operational automation and controlled endpoint administration.

MIMOC is designed for Windows-oriented business environments where IT teams need visibility, traceability and operational control across managed assets.

---

## What MIMOC does

MIMOC centralizes several IT operations in a single console:

- network discovery and asset detection;
- global inventory and operational CMDB;
- WMI Discovery for Windows assets;
- Software Scanner;
- Power Manager with Wake-on-LAN, reboot and shutdown actions;
- controlled Software Deployment;
- Task Scheduler;
- Credential Vault;
- audit log;
- backup, restore and maintenance mode.

Operational activities are handled through a backend queue and worker system, helping prevent large scans or automations from overloading the network, server or target endpoints.

---

## Philosophy

MIMOC is not just an inventory tool.

It is an IT orchestration console designed to:

- reduce tool fragmentation;
- improve CMDB data quality;
- make administrative actions traceable;
- control operational load;
- reduce risk during large-scale operations.

## Permitted use

MIMOC must be used only on:

- systems you own;
- authorized business networks;
- endpoints for which you have administrative or operational permission;
- test, lab or authorized production environments.

Do not use MIMOC to scan, access, automate or administer systems or networks without authorization.

---

## Important notice

MIMOC can perform high-impact operations, including:

- network scans;
- WMI, WinRM, SSH and SNMP queries;
- Wake-on-LAN;
- reboot and shutdown operations;
- software deployment;
- scheduled automation;
- credential management;
- database restore.

You are responsible for configuration, permissions, backups, credentials and compliance with applicable laws and internal policies.
